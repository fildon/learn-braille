What is this?

This app will teach you English Braille. Every braille symbol has a corresponding representation in text. You will be presented either a braille symbol, and four possible answers. Click on the correct answer to proceed. You will then be presented a new symbol or text representation. The app will remember which ones you got right and which ones you got wrong. Over time you will be presented with the ones you get wrong more often, until gradually you learn them all.
